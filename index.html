Design and Me

Bolton born & based creative David Entwistle, has had a passion for Art & Design from a young age. Making waves since his first pack of Crayolaâ€™s! Back in 2005 at the age of 9, he started animating with the now prehistoric Macromedia Flash. In his early teens this evolved into learning the basics of Adobe CS3/4 at after school Media Club. Soon after, David began to master Adobe CS5 and the use of Adobe Premiere Pro & Illustrator. Not long after, he was collaborating with YouTube creatives & producing various graphic & video content for different channels. Late teens and early 20's, the use of 3D printing perfected David's CAD/CAM skills and the use of mixed media through college inspired David's passion for combining traditional fine art with graphical abstract designs. Most recently he has been involved with designing graphics for Bolton FM Radio Station.
